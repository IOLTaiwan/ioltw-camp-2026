Program Highlights

Four thematic modules that systematically explore the logic behind language

🎧
The Hidden Code of Sound
Crack linguistic patterns through listening and uncover the rules hidden in sound systems
🧩
Sentence Stacking
Break down and rebuild languages from around the world while exploring the rich variety of grammatical structures
🔐
Decoding Hour
Take on real puzzle-solving challenges, crack linguistic codes, and experience the excitement of the Linguistics Olympiad
🏆
Olympian's Survival Guide
Experienced Olympiad participants share tips, strategies, and insights into the path toward the international IOL stage
